ABOUT MONALISA

Monalisa Thakali holds the distinction of being Nepal’s first Thakali restaurant, pioneering the introduction of authentic Thakali cuisine to a broader audience. Specializing in the traditional Thakali Khana Seta generous platter featuring dal, rice, seasonal vegetables, pickles, and regional specialtiesthe restaurant has become a beloved institution that preserves and celebrates the rich culinary heritage of the Thakali people from Nepal’s Mustang region.

Through its commitment to authentic flavors and warm hospitality, Monalisa Thakali has played an instrumental role in popularizing this distinctive Himalayan cuisine.
